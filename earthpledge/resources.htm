I pledge allegiance to the Earth and to life that it sustains.

One planet, in our care, irreplaceable with enough sustenance for the needs of all.

 

This Earth Pledge is a variation from the original created by: Janina Lamb

The urgency of seeing our world come together is driven by the obvious task of avoiding the unfavorable transition outlined by the two most widely influential and recognized paradigms in the west concerning humanity's fate. The first is the Biblical paradigm. In its outline for the future, our civilization is a doomed entity. Nothing can stop the Earth's blinded leaders from pushing us beyond the point of no return. They perpetuate business as usual preparing for the next world war and then waging it. The only thing that saves us from destroying more than a third of humanity is a transformed super Jesus who returns to govern a united world at peace, because we failed to achieve it on our own.

The other popularized vision for our future is the Star Trek paradigm. Although both the religious and secular scenarios end with a united human race governed in peace and prosperity, both of them have a horrendous transition period between now and then. Even in the Star Trek timeline, World War III is unavoidable and six hundred million people are murdered in this catastrophic event.

What we must now work towards is a new paradigm for our future; one in which we revise the horrific means that inaugurate its peaceful ends. This could help inspire a grassroots movement where citizens of the world unite and work together to plan for the prevention of World War III while leading us into a heroic, peaceful and accelerated transition towards a global federal government.

This unified end state of human governance finds compelling models in the successful cooperation between America's states and potentially the nations of the European Union. For over a century the U.S. has stood as a blueprint for success because war hasn't been waged within its borders. Citizens in California have never paid taxes to engage in a costly nuclear arms race with Texas. Californians spend nothing to maintain a golden state military or to finance a war on terror against religious fanatics in Utah. Alliances with New York, New England and the other states are still strong considering that billions of dollars have been freed up on the west coast; they never had to engage in an unpopular and costly invasion of Alabama to take out a brutal dictator feared to have weapons of mass destruction. The explosion of creativity and progress in the United States over the last 100 years was made possible by its people being united and members of one national family.

The significance of this model will only be realized if it leads us to cooperate with the international community and build closer alliances for making plans of a brighter future as a united states of earth. Just as Americans owe their prosperity and freedom to the founding fathers, who responded to the call of a great nation whose destiny they anticipated; future generations of our coming one world, are waiting on us to become vehicles of its fruition.

Become a world citizen and help spread an idea whose time has come.
