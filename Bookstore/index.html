How to Think About War and Peace by Mortimer J. Adler. A look at the basic ideas that make global government necessary.

Manifesto for a New World Order by George Monbiot. Proposes a world parliament and international organizations for fair trade.

One World: The Ethics of Globalization by Peter Singer (2nd edition). A provocative look at global warming, poverty and other pressing global concerns.

Bringing the Biosphere Home: Learning to Perceive Global Environmental Change by Mitchell Thomashow. How can you experience the biosphere in your particular place?
