Jane Shevtsov
Jane Shevtsov, co-founder of World Beyond Borders, does research and curriculum development in mathematical biology at UCLA. Originally from Ukraine (which was then part of the Soviet Union), she came with her family to the US at the age of 7 and grew up in Los Angeles, earning a B.S. in Ecology, Behavior and Evolution from UCLA in 2005 and a Ph.D. in systems ecology from the University of Georgia in 2012. Jane is an avid reader, is active on Google+, and enjoys rock wall climbing.
